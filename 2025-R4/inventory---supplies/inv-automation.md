# Use mobile devices to pick or ship sales orders in Sage Intacct Inventory Automation

[Sage Intacct Inventory Automation](https://www.intacct.com/ia/docs/en_US/help_action/Default.htm#cshid=inventory-automation)

INVENTORY CONTROL

Streamline your processes by picking or shipping sales orders from a mobile device.

## Key benefits

-   Automatically convert a sales order to a shipper with the touch of a button on a mobile screen.
-   Real-time data validation ensures that the correct items and quantities are shipped.
-   Optional settings provide more flexibility by letting you do the following:
    -   Ship non-inventory items.
    -   Add items to a sales order that were not originally on it.

For more information about Sage Intacct Inventory Automation, talk to your [Sage Intacct account manager](https://www.intacct.com/ia/docs/en_US/help_action/Default.htm#cshid=Contact_your_account_manager) or visit the [Sage Intacct Inventory Automation web page](https://www.sage.com/en-us/sage-business-cloud/intacct/resources/datasheets/streamline-tracking-inventory-with-inventory-automation/).
