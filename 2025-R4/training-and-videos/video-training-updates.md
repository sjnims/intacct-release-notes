# New training and videos

training and videos

At Sage Intacct, we get how vital it is to have in-depth and best practice learning that enhance your use of Intacct. To that end we recently:

-   Provided access to new and updated self-paced, on-demand courses that guide you through specific tasks and features.
-   Added new product demo videos to help you get up and running on a variety of tasks.

## New course format coming soon

The Sage Intacct Fundamentals training is transitioning into 5 separate courses giving you more flexibility to complete the topics that apply best to your role. These new courses will be:

-   Managing Journal Entries and the General Ledger (NA and UK)
    
-   Managing Cash (NA and UK)
    
-   Running Reports in Sage Intacct (NA and UK)
    
-   Processing Accounts Receivable (NA and UK)
    
-   Processing Accounts Payable (NA and UK)
