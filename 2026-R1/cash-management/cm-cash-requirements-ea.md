# Introducing Cash requirements with expanded currency support—Early Adopter

[About Cash requirements](https://preview.intacct.com/ia/docs/en_US/help_action/Default.htm#cshid=About_cash_requirements)

Cash Management

Take our new Cash requirements feature for a test-drive and join the Early adopter program. Cash requirements now supports all available currencies, with improved short-term payables forecasting.

View amounts for payables, receivables, and bank balances on a single page to help efficiently surface cash flow insights.

Cash requirements is in testing and the data shown might not reflect your complete accounting records. Do not rely on this information for financial, tax, or business decisions.

## What's changed

- **Expanded currency support**: Cash requirements now supports additional currencies and is available to any single-currency company in all regions. Previously, it was only available to single-currency companies operating in USD.
- **Improved payables forecasting**: With this release, Cash requirements uses a predictive model and real-time data to project the next 1-4 weeks of expected AP outflows.

  For more information, go to [About Cash requirements](https://www.intacct.com/ia/docs/en_US/help_action/Cash_Management/Cash_requirements/about-cash-requirements-ns.htm?cshid=About_cash_requirements).

## Key benefits

- **Optimized cash management**: See cash balance and forecasted change to understand your weekly cash position.
- **AI-powered cash analysis**: Leverage Copilot insights to quickly interpret short-term cash requirements and risks.
- **Faster payment decisions**: Identify potential shortfalls impacting upcoming cash obligations to prioritize payments with confidence.

## How it works

Access Cash requirements at the entity level.

### Step 1. Grant permissions to view the Cash requirements page

1. Go to **Company** > **Admin** > **Users, roles, and groups** > **Users**.
2. Find the user that you want to enable for Cash requirements.
3. Select More actions > **Edit** at the end of the row.
4. Select **Subscriptions**.
5. Find the Cash Management application and select **Permissions**.
6. Find the Cash requirements permission and select **View**.  
    This permission only appears after your Early Adopter access has begun.
7. Select **Save**.

### Step 2. Grant permissions to view data

Grant permissions for AP, AR, and bank account data so the Cash requirements page can display balances and calculate estimates.

Permissions need to be granted at the entity level. Refer to the Permissions and other requirements table below. For more information, go to [Assign permissions to users](https://preview.intacct.com/ia/docs/en_US/help_action/Default.htm#cshid=Assign_permissions_users).

### Step 3. View Cash requirements

1. Go to **Cash Management** > **All** > **Cash requirements**.
2. Select **Cash requirements**.

## What's the Early Adopter program?

Early Adopter programs give participants access to features before they're available to everyone. Early adopters are expected to respond to feedback and survey requests. As an early adopter, you can influence how we develop the product so that it best meets your business needs.

If you want to be considered for the Early Adopter program, [complete this form](https://forms.office.com/r/5MqxeYXmYd) today.
