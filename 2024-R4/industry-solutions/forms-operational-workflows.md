# Enhancements in Sage Intacct Forms and Operational Workflows

-   Validate federal ID numbers, names, and addresses with matching through TaxBandits.
-   Easily reproduce fillable PDF forms by mapping fields from your Sage Intacct data to fields in the PDF.
