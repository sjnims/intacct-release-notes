# Prevent errors when creating a bank or credit card account

[Add a checking account](https://www.intacct.com/ia/docs/en_US/help_action/Default.htm#cshid=Adding_Editing_and_Viewing_Checking_Accounts)

[Edit a checking account](https://www.intacct.com/ia/docs/en_US/help_action/Default.htm#cshid=Edit_checking_account)

cash management

Now, when creating a bank or credit card account, you cannot save the record if you have selected an account configured as the default Accounts Payable or Accounts Receivable account. The best practice is to select a cash, cash equivalent, or credit card liability account.
