# Define rates for billing—Sage Intelligent Time

With rate cards, you can now set clear and consistent billing rates for employees, items, or projects. This approach ensures a transparent and uniform pricing structure, streamlining the billing process for greater efficiency.

[![The Rate cards list.](https://www.intacct.com/ia/docs/en_US/releasenotes/Resources/Images/2025/2025-R1-images/2025-R1-SIT-rate-cards_thumb_0_100.png)](https://www.intacct.com/ia/docs/en_US/releasenotes/Resources/Images/2025/2025-R1-images/2025-R1-SIT-rate-cards.png)

## Details

Rate cards create transparent and consistent pricing structures, making it easier for clients to understand costs upfront. This streamlined pricing process reduces the time and effort needed to negotiate rates for individual services. Additionally, rate cards are highly flexible and can be customized per person, item, or at the project level.​

## How it works

### Create a rate card

1.  Go to **Projects** > **All** > **Rate cards**.
    
2.  Select **Create rate card**.
    
3.  Select or enter an **Effective date**.
    
4.  Enter a **Name**.
    
5.  Choose a **Project**. (Optional)
    
6.  Select **Create**.
    
7.  Select **New entry**.
    
8.  Choose an **Employee**.
    
9.  Select an **Item**. (Optional)
    
10.  Enter a **Rate**.
     
     You can select **New entry** to include a new entry rate.
     
11.  **Save** your changes.
     
12.  Select **Close**.
