# New training and videos

training and videos

At Sage Intacct, we get how vital it is to have in-depth and best practice learning that enhance your use of Intacct. To that end we recently:

-   Provided access to new and updated self-paced, on-demand courses that guide you through specific tasks and features.
-   Added new product demo videos to help you get up and running on a variety of tasks.

## New and updated training courses

You can find these new and updated training courses at [Sage University](https://sageu.com/SageIntacct/).

You'll need to [log in to Sage University with your Sage account](https://www.intacct.com/ia/docs/en_US/help_action/Default.htm#cshid=Connect_Sage_University).

| Application | Course | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Projects | Recognizing Revenue with Projects | Learn about revenue management with Sage Intacct Projects. Review the Order Entry revenue management workflow options, creating revenue activation documents, billing and revenue recognition workflows, Projects reports, and troubleshooting. |
| Processing Expenses with Projects | Teaches project users about the functions of expenses in Sage Intacct Projects. Review the employee record, adding users, creating expense reports, approval workflow for expenses, reimbursing employee expenses, reporting on expenses, and troubleshooting. |
| Reporting | Getting Started with Custom Report Writer | Teaches the basics of creating a report with the Custom Report Writer. Learn how to add columns, sort and group information, and filter data using the Custom Report Writer. |
| Time and Expenses | Processing Expenses with Projects | Teaches project users about the functions of expenses in Sage Intacct Projects. Review the employee record, adding users, creating expense reports, approval workflow for expenses, reimbursing employee expenses, reporting on expenses, and troubleshooting. |
| Nonprofit | Qualified expenses and Invoicing with Projects and Grants | Teaches project and grant users about qualified expenses and invoicing in Sage Intacct Projects and Grants. Review the invoicing workflow options, creating qualified expense transactions, Project and Grant billing reports, and troubleshooting. |
| Recognizing Revenue with Projects and Grants | Learn about revenue management with Sage Intacct Projects and Grants. Review the Order Entry revenue management workflow options, creating revenue activation documents, billing and revenue recognition workflows, Projects reports, and troubleshooting. |
