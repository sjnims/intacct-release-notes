# Instance Setup Wizard enhancements

We've updated the setup wizard to allow for a smoother experience when creating companies. We've removed fields and tabs that are no longer in use and consolidation the remaining fields on the Company and Login tabs.

## Added

### Company tab

- Time zone

### Login tab

- Contact phone
- Contact first name
- Contact last name
- Contact name
- Contact email

## Removed

- Legal tab
- Payment tab
- Contact tab
- Accrual and cash option
