# Simplify cost and grant reporting with one-click downloads

Easily assemble project and grant attachments to share with customers or grant funding sources.

-   **One-click download:** Select and compile source documents into a single zip file for streamlined reporting.
    
-   **Prove qualified expenses:** Demonstrate cost-plus and grant-qualified expense billings with ease.
    
-   **Expanded compatibility:** Now included with Project Costing & Billing, Grant Tracking & Billing, and Construction subscriptions.
