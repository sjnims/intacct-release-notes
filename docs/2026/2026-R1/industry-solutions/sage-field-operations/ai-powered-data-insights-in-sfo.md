# Artificial Intelligence data insights in Sage Field Operations

[About Sage Field Operations](https://preview.intacct.com/ia/docs/en_US/help_action/Default.htm#cshid=sage-field-operations)

Sage Field Operations

We're excited to introduce AI capabilities in BI (Business Intelligence) Solution Views to help you understand your data faster and more effectively. AI-generated summaries and recommendations now appear directly in your data views, so you do not need to manually analyze charts and tables. The AI instantly highlights key trends and insights for you.

## Key benefits

- **Intelligent summaries**: Get simple explanations of what your data is showing, highlighting the most important patterns and trends.
- **Anomaly detection**: Automatically identify red flags and unusual patterns that might require attention.
- **Action recommendations**: Receive specific, contextualized suggestions for areas of improvement based on your data.
- **Performance insights**: Understand which areas of your business are performing well and areas that might need focus.

## Why this matters

Analyzing complex business intelligence data can be time-consuming and requires significant expertise to identify meaningful patterns. This new AI capability gives you a faster and clear understanding of your data and helps make informed decisions so that you can avoid manually reviewing every metric.
