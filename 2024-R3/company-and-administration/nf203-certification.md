# NF203 certification for France

We're thrilled to announce that Sage Intacct has achieved NF203 certification. This recognition attests to the reliability, security, and traceability of accounting data maintained in Sage Intacct according to French legislation.

## Benefits

NF203 certification offers the following assurances to Sage Intacct customers:

- **Enhanced security:** Your financial data is protected by cutting-edge security measures.
- **Increased reliability:** You can count on stable and reliable performance that's essential for daily business operations.
- **Regulatory compliance:** We're committed to meeting French standards and regulations while ensuring that your data is immutable, traceable, and properly archived.

## What's NF203 certification?

NF203 certification is issued by INFOCERT—an independent certification body specializing in software certification. INFOCERT performs a rigorous evaluation to ensure that accounting software complies with French regulatory requirements.

For more information, visit the [NF203 Computerized Accounting](https://infocert.org/en/nf203-comptabilite-informatisee/) page on the INFOCERT website.
