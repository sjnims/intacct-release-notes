Requires the Construction subscription.

-   Description
    
-   Net change retainage held
    
-   Retainage held to date
    
-   Retainage billed to date
    
-   Retainage balance to date
    
-   Previous retainage balance
    
-   Balance to finish
    
-   Line amount due
