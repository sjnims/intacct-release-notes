# Enhancements in Sage Intacct Planning

[Basic budgeting vs. budgeting and Planning](https://www.intacct.com/ia/docs/en_US/help_action/Default.htm#cshid=Basic_vs_budgeting_and_planning)

Budgeting and Planning

Sage Intacct Planning (SIP) is an add-on application that offers a collaborative environment for preparing and managing budgets.

If you have not subscribed to Sage Intacct Planning, talk to your Sage Intacct account manager for information about [subscribing](https://www.intacct.com/ia/docs/en_US/help_action/Default.htm#cshid=Contact_your_account_manager).

## Recent highlights

- Use the budget collaboration indicators to see who is currently working on your shared budget at any given moment. Hover over the initials on the collaboration indicators (or select the green arrow icon if one appears) on the main toolbar to list the online contributors.

  To learn more about this feature, see [Budget collaboration indicators](https://help.intacct-planning.com/intacct-planning/SIP_Topics/Budget_collaboration_indicators.htm).

  ![List of people who are currently working on the budget.](https://www.intacct.com/ia/docs/en_US/releasenotes/Resources/Images/2024/2024-R1-images/2024-R1-SI-planning-view-budget-contributors.png)

- Use the **Save as file** option to download and save all of your models and statistical accounts as a BDGM file. You can then upload the file to other budgets. For more information, see [Save and upload your models and statistical accounts](https://help.intacct-planning.com/intacct-planning/Default.htm#cshid=Accounts).
- Create custom views in Sheets to see your data in the way that works best for you. To learn about this feature, see [Custom views in Sheets](https://help.intacct-planning.com/intacct-planning/Default.htm#cshid=Sheets_custom_views).

  ![The Custom Views menu showing a list of views. ](https://www.intacct.com/ia/docs/en_US/releasenotes/Resources/Images/2024/2024-R1-images/2024-R1-si-planning-custom_views.png)
